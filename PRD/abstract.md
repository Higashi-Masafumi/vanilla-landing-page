# Abstract

## Project Name
Receipt Share (レシートシェア)

## Overview
A mobile application that simplifies bill splitting by automatically scanning receipts, performing OCR, and sharing them within a group.

## Core Features
1.  **Smart Scan & OCR**: Instantly digitize receipts with high accuracy.
2.  **Group Sharing**: Create groups for trips, events, or shared living and share receipts in real-time.
3.  **Automatic Bill Splitting**: Automatically calculate who owes what based on the shared receipts. No more manual calculations.

## Target Audience
- Friends traveling together.
- Roommates sharing expenses.
- Couples managing shared finances.
- Event organizers.

## Landing Page Goal
To clearly communicate the value proposition of "Receipt Share" and encourage users to download the app (or sign up for a waitlist).

## Design Direction
- **Modern & Clean**: Use a clean aesthetic that emphasizes ease of use. Base colors: White & Blue.
- **Rich Animations**: Utilize Three.js for immersive 3D effects and smooth transitions.
- **Mobile-First**: The design should look great on mobile devices as it's a mobile app companion.
- **Visuals**: Utilize the provided iPhone 16 Pro mockups to showcase the app interface.
